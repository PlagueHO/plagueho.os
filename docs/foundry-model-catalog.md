## Summary

This catalog snapshot covers all models available in the Microsoft Azure AI Foundry model
catalog as of the last-updated date. Models are grouped into three collections: **Azure Direct**
(models sold directly by Azure with global standard deployment across up to 30 Azure regions),
**Partners and Community** (marketplace models from trusted third-party providers), and
**Azure OpenAI** (OpenAI models available through Azure AI Foundry). The catalog includes
111 models across 11 publishers, spanning chat-completion, embeddings, image-generation,
speech, audio, video, and document-AI capabilities.
